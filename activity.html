THE MERCY MINISTRY

AT THE MOST BASIC LEVEL mercy ministry is helping people with a practical need in the name of Jesus Christ ,this can take many forms and can be achieved in different ways
But why do we engage in mercy ministry? For many of us , we have experienced the challanges of poverty or the physical pain of an ailment that interrupted our lives and so we want others to experiencejoy too....Feeeding programmes to the vulnerable and less fortunate in the community and the widows and orphans and street children this helps in reconstruction and restoring hope in the community

Jesus calls us to care for those in need and to give in His name because when we do these things we are doing them unto Him
